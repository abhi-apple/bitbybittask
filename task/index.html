About Us

The “Bit By Bit” student coding club aims to help young aspirants follow a practical-based learning path and provide them with abundant educational opportunities through Interactive workshops, coding competitions, training camps, and other exhilarating activities ensuring each member of the team gain unparalleled coding experience and knowledge.

The student members and faculties truly believe in developing and inculcating a strong competitive programming culture among young programmers.The club aims to help its student members acquire knowledge of cutting-edge technologies along with gaining crucial interpersonal values in order for them to become proficient and versatile computer programmers.
